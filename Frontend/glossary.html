Terms and Definitions

Simple explanations for common income and tax related terms.

Assessment Period

The monthly period used to calculate Universal Credit.

Benefit Cap

A limit on the total amount of benefits some households can receive.

Council Tax Reduction

Help with reducing your council tax bill.

Housing Benefit

Support to help eligible people pay rent.

Sanction

A reduction in benefits when rules or commitments are not met.

Universal Credit

A payment to help with living costs for eligible people.
